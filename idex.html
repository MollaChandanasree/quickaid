Quick Aid - Emergency Response System

  • 📱 Provide a fast and reliable way for people to report medical emergencies via SMS or Voice command.
  • 🏥 Improve Access to Healthcare between patients, responders, and hospitals, especially in rural areas.
  • 🚑 Enhance Coordination and communication between ambulances, hospitals, and first responders for faster response times.
  • 💡 Provide Critical First-Aid Information via automated SMS or app-based guidance on basic first-aid procedures while waiting for medical help.
  • 📞 Design a user-friendly system that works on any mobile device, including basic phones.
  • 🛏 Help hospitals and responders track available beds, medical supplies, and specialist availability in real time.
  • 📢 Educate users on emergency protocols and health tips through SMS-based health updates and reminders.
